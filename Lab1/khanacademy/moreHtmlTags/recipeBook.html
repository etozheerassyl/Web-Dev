Shaker Apple Pie

  • Time: 45 minut
  • Serves: me
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 5
sliced apple 500 gm
dough 1
brown sugar 1 spoon

Step 1: make the apple pie filling by mixing the ingredients in a large bowl. No pre-cooking of the apples is necessary for this pie.

Step 2: roll out bottom pie dough and line the pie dish with the crust.

Step 3: roll out and make the top crust. I share how to make this lattice crust below this section. Alternately, keep it simple! Roll out the top crust into a full circle and use that, cutting a few vents prior to baking to allow steam to escape. Seal and crimp.
